e-Learning

This course provides an overview of the epidemiology and pathophysiology of cervical dystonia. It elaborates on patient assessment, diagnosis and goal setting. It briefly introduces the BOTOX® injection paradigm and includes links to useful clinic resources.

The module features expert opinion videos from Dr Andrew Evans (Specialist Neurologist, Royal Melbourne Hospital, VIC) covering patient assessment, diagnosis and injecting with BOTOX®.

This activity is for you, if:

  • You inject BOTOX® infrequently or < 10 times a year.
  • • You are looking for tips and tricks on patient assessment, patient selection and goal setting for BOTOX® injection for cervical dystonia.
